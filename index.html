Why Accessibility?

I'm interested in accessibility because as someone who is interested in front-end development and UI/UX design, I've learned that accessibility is essential for creating inclusive digital experiences that work for everyone, regardless of their abilities or disabilities.

There are so many details and nuances to these building these experiences that get overlooked.
My goals are to better understand the disabilities that are out there, the needs and experiences of user populations with those disabilities, and better understand the laws and standards that govern accessibility.

I want to explore Web Accessibility, Educational/Instructional Accessibility, and Universal Design.
